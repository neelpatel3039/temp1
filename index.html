Problem Statement

There are enough examples the world has shown us to validate the statement - "tum passion ko follow karu, success jakh maar ke tumhare peeche aegi" From humongously wealthy businessmen to film stars to other majorly pursued professions like that of doctors, engineers, CAs, etc everywhere we will see people who love the field succeding.

This tells us the choosing the right field is very important. One might think, so what's the problem, select the field of passion and boom! you know you'll succeed. ...

As students, we came across this difficulty which we had while choosing the streams we wanted to have careers in. The problem is that the traditional question - "Science? Commerce? or Arts?" is not the right question to ask a student who is not even mature enough to understand that these are very broad areas and they have so many layers of complexity in them. Practically viewing, there are many facets when it comes to deciding the first step out of school [10th boards]. And no doubt this lack of clarity towards (1) what is the right way one should make study-related decisions? (2) how to decide what we really want to do?

There exist a solution to this. At least it is something that can bring a student 5-10% closer to what they really want to choose. We also thought about it, and then sort of formulated a concept which is evident in quite a few places but is not viewed from a perspective we are looking at it. We have named it - backward approach to initial decision making.

Our Model

Backward Approach - backward approach to initial decision making or backward approach in short says once decide the kind of lifestyle you genuinely want and then accordingly plan backwards & then decide what all things you'll have to do to reach there.

We basically offer services which help students & parents implement this backward approach and dont fall short of knowlegde resources & other activity based techniques.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Giving knowledge that helps parents and students of 8, 9, and 10 to make better initial decisions in the career aspect.


Mission

[1]. Providing relevant, dynamic, and easily understandable knowledge related to initial decision making of Mumbai based 8, 9, and 10 students and their parents.
[2]. Becoming a platform to connect the parents, teachers, and students of 8, 9, and 10th to the industry professions to know the ground report of specific things they might be very concerned about.
